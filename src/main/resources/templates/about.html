The Seeds of Our Story

Fresh Farm Juba was born out of a simple necessity: the lack of reliable, fresh, and organic produce in the local markets of Juba.

What started as a small family initiative to provide clean poultry and vegetables for our neighbors has grown into a modern agricultural hub. Today, we utilize sustainable irrigation and organic fertilizers to ensure that every broiler, layer, and cabbage meets the highest standards of quality.
